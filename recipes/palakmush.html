Palak Mushroom

Description

Palak Mushroom is a healthy and delicious dish of sauteed button mushrooms and aromatics in a creamy spinach sauce. Loaded with the goodness of spinach and mushrooms you are sure to like this dish. Make it for a hearty weekend lunch or dinner to mop it with some soft roti, crispy paratha or enjoy it with fragrant cumin rice.

Ingredients

  • 250 gms Spinach leaves
  • 3 green chilli
  • Salt
  • 1tsp sugar
  • 2l Water
  • 200 gms Mushrooms
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • 2 Red Chilli
  • 1 tsp Cumin
  • 2 tbsp Chopped Garlic (10 small ones)
  • 1/4 cup Chopped Onions
  • 1/4 tsp Turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1/2 tsp Chilli powder
  • 1/2 tsp Roasted Cumin powder
  • 1 tsp Kasuri Methi
  • 2 tbsp Fresh cream
  • 125 ml Water

Steps

  1. Boil chopped Spinach, Green Chillies, Salt, Sugar and boil them once and put them in cold water immediately.
  2. Boil Mushrooms in a separate pan and put them in cold water and keep them aside.
  3. Heat some oil in a pan, fry Red chillies and Cumin wait till they splutter.
  4. Now add grated Garlic and fry till they turn light brown then add chopped Onions and fry till they turn golden brown.
  5. Once it is properly fried add Salt, Turmeric and Spinach paste. Cook till the oil floats to the top.
  6. Add Chilli powder, Garam masala, Kasuri Methi and fry for some more time, till the oil leaves the Spinach.
  7. Add water to it and once. Add Mushrooms to it and fry till the oil floats to the top.
  8. Finally, add Fresh Cream, stir well and remove from flame.
